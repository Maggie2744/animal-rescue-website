ABOUT US

Ottawa Dogs & Cats Rescue is a volunteer operated organization that gives animals a second chance at living in a loving home.

Through generous donations and hours upon hours of volunteer time, we have become Ottawa’s leading rescue and adoption agency.

Please feel free to contact us at anytime with any inquiries that you may have about rescuing and adoption.
